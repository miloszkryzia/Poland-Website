The Polish Language

Język Polski

Polish is an indoeuropean slavic language.

That means it is similar to languages like Russian or Ukrainian.

It uses the Latin script, same as English or Spanish,

with several additional letters: ą, ć, ę, ł, ń, ó, ś, ź, ż.

Polish is considered to be one of the hardest languages to learn, beacuse of its

many conjugations, complicated spelling and difficult pronunciation.

For example:

"ruthless" - "bezwzględny"

"happiness" - "szczęście"

There are many words that should be easy to pronounce for Spanish speakers though.
